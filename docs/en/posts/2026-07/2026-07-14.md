# The Leftover Premium Women

A highly eligible man who is wealthy, handsome, and successful would rather date a girl you think is plain or has worse conditions than be with you, who considers yourself far more superior and excellent—if so, you must have some major issues. We believe we have a developed industrial system, are a infrastructure giant, and have low costs, yet many manufacturers, such as Apple and Foxconn, would rather go to India, which we perceive to be worse, than come to China. Why do you think that is?

Apple assembled approximately 36 million iPhones in India in 2024, a figure that increased to 55 million in 2025, accounting for 25% of its global output. Industry forecasts project India's share will rise to 26%–28% by the end of 2026, taking over the manufacturing of the majority of iPhones sold in the U.S. market. Accompanying this shift, Foxconn committed $1.5 billion in fresh investment to its Indian operations in mid-2025 and infused an additional $37.2 million into its subsidiary in June 2026, bringing its cumulative investment near Bengaluru to $2.6 billion.
