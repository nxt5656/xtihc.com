# I'm back

The domain was banned because I told stories about our village chief on the blog. After continuous efforts, I transferred the domain to Cloudflare.

I will still say what I want to say. If I am not allowed to say it here, I will find another place to keep saying it.

<br>

### 2026-07-08 (Supplement)

My domain was previously dedicated strictly to writing technical blogs, and I originally had no plans to write about any shortcomings of the government on this blog. I believe that even if I were placed in a similar position of authority, I might also practice nepotism and prioritize the interests of myself and those around me when faced with power and benefits.

However, I previously posted an article recounting an incident from my trip back to my hometown: an old Party member with fifty years of standing told me that all of the village's assets had been lost, and many of them had become the personal property of the village leaders or their relatives. The old Party member was extremely saddened and wept while telling me this. I simply recorded this statement in my article.

Following this, our village Party Secretary called me and demanded that I delete the article and deregister my domain. He stated that if I did not comply, he would travel to Hangzhou to detain me. At the same time, he called several of my relatives, stating that if I did not do as he said, the political background checks (Zhengshen) and jobs of all my relatives would be affected. In particular, he threatened my brother, stating that if my brother could not persuade me to delete the article and deregister the domain, my brother's two children would not be allowed to attend school. Subsequently, he used his personal connections and channels to have my domestic domain name blocked.

I do not understand: is it no longer allowed to speak and state objective facts?

This village Party Secretary is **Niu Changchun, the Party Secretary of Niutan Village, Yingshui Town, Shapotou District, Zhongwei City, Ningxia**.

After this incident, some villagers sent me messages and videos regarding his other behaviors in the village, but I did not post any of them on my blog. Since those were not my personal experiences and I could not fully verify them, I felt there was no need to publish them. On this blog, I only record the facts that I have personally experienced and verified.
